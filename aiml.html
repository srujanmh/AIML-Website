Welcome to the Department of Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Shaping the future of technology through innovation, education, and research.

1. About the Department

The Department of Computer Science is a vibrant hub of academic excellence and cutting-edge research. Established with a vision to impart quality education and foster a spirit of innovation, we offer a comprehensive range of programs designed to equip students with the skills required to excel in the dynamic field of technology. Our dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and strong industry collaborations create an environment where students can thrive and realize their full potential.

2. HoD’s Message

"It is with immense pleasure that I welcome you to the Department of Computer Science. In an era where technology is rapidly evolving, our goal is to not only keep pace but to be at the forefront of this change. We are committed to providing a rich learning experience that combines rigorous academics with practical, hands-on application. Our curriculum is designed to challenge students, encouraging critical thinking and problem-solving skills. I am confident that our graduates will be the leaders and innovators of tomorrow, driving progress in various industries."

3. Vision, Mission and Objectives of the Department

Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in computer science education and research, fostering innovation and social responsibility.

Mission

To provide a strong theoretical foundation, practical skills, and ethical values to students, enabling them to become competent professionals and lifelong learners.

Objectives

  • Offer high-quality academic programs.
  • Promote interdisciplinary research.
  • Encourage industry collaboration.
  • Develop holistic personalities.
